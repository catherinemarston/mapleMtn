WE ARE A GROUP OF PASSIONATE BREWERS LOCATED IN PROVO, UTAH.

We handcraft beers in small 150-gallon batches under the close attention of our experienced brewers. MMBC recipes contain only the highest quality ingredients including organic, local (as available), and non-gmo products.

These standards combined with the unique process of using only purified sunbaked water and traditional brewing methods, ensures a level of quality that shines no matter the method of storage (bottle, can, keg).
